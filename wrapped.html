Connect Your Health Data for a Complete Picture

Go Go Gaia pulls from Apple Health to include all your existing health data in your year-in-review. Here's how to make sure everything is connected.

Period Tracker Data

Using Flo, Clue, or another period tracker? Make sure it's syncing to Apple Health so your cycle history is included in your Health Wrapped. Go to your app's settings → Apple Health → enable cycle tracking sync.

Nutrition Data

Track food with MyFitnessPal, Cronometer, or Lose It? Connect them to Apple Health so your nutrition patterns appear in your Health Wrapped alongside your cycle and mood data.

Wearable Data

Wear a Garmin, Fitbit, Oura Ring, or WHOOP? Sync your device to Apple Health to include steps, sleep, heart rate, and activity in your Health Wrapped year-in-review.

Apple Watch

Apple Watch data automatically syncs to Apple Health. Your steps, workouts, heart rate, and sleep will all be included in your Health Wrapped with no extra setup needed.

Sleep Data

Use a sleep tracking app or device? Whether it's Sleep Cycle, AutoSleep, Oura, or your Apple Watch, make sure sleep data is syncing to Apple Health for complete insights.

Workout Data

Log workouts in Strava, Nike Run Club, Peloton, or other fitness apps? Connect them to Apple Health so your exercise patterns and cycle correlations show up in your Wrapped.
