Understanding Your Cycle

Just starting to track your menstrual cycle? We make it simple to understand your body's natural rhythms and hormonal changes throughout the month.

Learn when to expect your period with accurate cycle predictions, identify your cycle phases (follicular, ovulation, luteal, and menstrual), and discover how your hormones affect your mood, energy levels, and overall wellbeing. Track PMS symptoms, monitor period flow and length, and understand ovulation patterns. Perfect for anyone wanting to practice cycle syncing—aligning your lifestyle, workouts, and nutrition with your menstrual cycle phases for better productivity, performance, and self-care. Whether you're new to period tracking or want deeper insights into your hormonal health, Go Go Gaia helps you decode your body's signals.

Managing PCOS & Hormonal Health

Living with PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) or irregular periods? Track symptoms, identify triggers, and take control of your hormonal health with comprehensive PCOS management tools.

Monitor irregular menstrual cycles and unpredictable periods, track PCOS symptoms including acne, hirsutism (excess hair growth), weight fluctuations, mood swings, anxiety, and depression. Log insulin resistance indicators, track your PCOS diet and supplements, monitor hormonal acne patterns, and record fertility treatments or medications like Metformin or birth control. Identify triggers and patterns in your PCOS symptoms to share with your endocrinologist or OBGYN. Our PCOS tracker helps you understand your unique hormonal imbalances and provides data-driven insights for better PCOS treatment and management. Perfect for women with diagnosed PCOS, suspected hormonal imbalances, or anyone navigating the challenges of polycystic ovary syndrome.

Trying to Conceive

Ready to start your family? We help you identify your most fertile days and optimize your chances of conception with advanced fertility tracking and ovulation prediction.

Track ovulation predictions and pinpoint your fertile window, log basal body temperature (BBT) for accurate ovulation detection, monitor cervical mucus changes throughout your cycle, and record LH surge results from ovulation predictor kits (OPKs). Get fertile window alerts so you never miss your most fertile days, track intimacy timing for conception planning, and monitor early pregnancy symptoms during the two-week wait (TWW). Perfect for couples trying to conceive (TTC), whether you're just starting your fertility journey or have been trying for several cycles. Our fertility tracker supports natural conception, helps you understand your ovulation cycle, and provides the data you need for conversations with fertility specialists or reproductive endocrinologists if needed. Compatible with IVF, IUI, and other assisted reproductive technology (ART) treatments.

Pregnancy Journey

Expecting? Track every precious moment of your pregnancy with week-by-week guidance, trimester milestones, and comprehensive prenatal monitoring.

Follow your baby's development week by week through all three trimesters, log pregnancy symptoms including morning sickness, nausea, fatigue, food aversions, and cravings. Monitor healthy pregnancy weight gain according to your pre-pregnancy BMI, count baby kicks and fetal movements in the third trimester, and keep track of prenatal appointments, ultrasounds, and prenatal vitamins. Track blood pressure readings, glucose levels for gestational diabetes monitoring, and Braxton Hicks contractions as you approach your due date. Record questions for your OBGYN or midwife, document pregnancy mood changes and emotions, and create a complete prenatal health record to share with your healthcare provider. Perfect for first-time moms and experienced mothers alike, whether you're navigating a high-risk pregnancy or enjoying a healthy, low-risk pregnancy journey. Cherish every moment from conception to delivery.

Perimenopause & Menopause

Navigating perimenopause or menopause? Track symptoms, understand hormonal changes, and take control of this natural life transition with comprehensive menopause tracking tools.

Monitor irregular periods and cycle changes as you transition into menopause, track hot flashes and night sweats frequency and intensity, log sleep disturbances and insomnia patterns, and record mood changes, anxiety, and depression. Track vaginal dryness, joint pain, brain fog, and memory issues. Monitor hormone replacement therapy (HRT) effects including estrogen, progesterone, and testosterone treatments. Log lifestyle changes like diet modifications, exercise routines, and supplements for menopause symptoms. Track perimenopause symptoms including spotting, heavy bleeding, and cycle length changes. Perfect for women in perimenopause (typically 40s), experiencing menopause (average age 51), or postmenopausal women managing ongoing symptoms. Share your comprehensive symptom data with your gynecologist or menopause specialist to optimize your treatment plan. Our tracking helps you identify triggers for hot flashes, understand patterns in your symptoms, and navigate this transition with confidence and clarity.
