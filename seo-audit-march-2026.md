## Key Insights

### What's Working
1. **Comparison content strategy** — The how-to-choose article alone accounts for 82% of all impressions
2. **Impression growth** — 24x increase in 3 months shows Google is discovering and testing your content
3. **Position improvement** — Moving from page 3-4 to page 1 on many queries
4. **Topic authority building** — Ranking for perimenopause (3.8), PCOS queries (6-8), ovulation (varies)

### What's Not Working
1. **CTR is critically low** — 0.17% on the top page means 99.83% of people see your result and don't click
2. **Brand queries not converting** — "go gaia" variants at position 6-9 should be position 1
3. **Comparison page self-cannibalizing** — Fragment URLs (#step-2, #comparison-table) get 4K+ impressions with 0 clicks
4. **Ovulation guide stuck** — Position 44 with 2K impressions means content needs a major overhaul
5. **Many pages at 0 clicks** — 13 of 22 blog posts have zero clicks despite having impressions

### Desktop vs Mobile Gap
- Mobile: 181 clicks / 32K impressions = 0.56% CTR
- Desktop: 101 clicks / 54K impressions = 0.19% CTR
- Desktop gets MORE impressions but FAR fewer clicks — meta descriptions may be getting truncated or not compelling enough on desktop SERPs

## International Opportunity
You're getting impressions from 200+ countries. The "translated results" search appearance has a 5.1% CTR — your BEST CTR by far. Consider adding hreflang tags for key languages (Spanish, Portuguese, German, French) where you have significant impression volume.

## Monthly Review Checklist
1. Export Search Console data (Queries, Pages, Countries, Devices)
2. Compare impression trends month-over-month
3. Identify new query clusters emerging
4. Check CTR changes on pages with updated titles/descriptions
5. Review position changes for target keywords
6. Look for new fragment URL cannibalization issues
