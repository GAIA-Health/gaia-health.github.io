How it fits together

Other apps track each thing in its own silo: pain in one place, cycle in another, sleep somewhere else entirely. Go Go Gaia keeps your cycle, symptoms, nutrition, sleep, and wearable data in one place, then surfaces real patterns from your own data, like a lifestyle factor that lines up with flare days. That connective layer is the same correlation engine behind every condition the app tracks, not a separate tool bolted on for endo.
