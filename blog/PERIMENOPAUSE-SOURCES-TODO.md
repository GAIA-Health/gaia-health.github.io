#### 4. Cognitive Changes During Perimenopause
**Claims to support:**
- Memory issues, brain fog, slower processing during perimenopause
- "These cognitive changes are distressing but typically improve after menopause"

**Where to find:**
- Neuroimaging studies on perimenopause and cognition
- Longitudinal cognitive testing studies
- PubMed search: "perimenopause cognitive function" "menopause transition memory"

**Suggested source:**

## üìö KEY ORGANIZATIONS & RESOURCES

### Authoritative Sources for Perimenopause Info:

1. **NAMS (North American Menopause Society)**
   - Website: menopause.org
   - Gold standard for menopause information
   - Position statements are peer-reviewed and evidence-based
   - **2022 Hormone Therapy Position Statement** is MUST-CITE

2. **ACOG (American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists)**
   - Practice Bulletins on perimenopause/menopause
   - Patient education resources
   - Clinical guidelines

3. **SWAN Study (Study of Women's Health Across the Nation)**
   - Largest longitudinal study of menopause transition
   - Multi-year follow-up of 3,000+ women
   - Published extensively on perimenopause symptoms, duration, timing
   - Search PubMed: "SWAN study" + [your topic]

4. **Women's Health Initiative (WHI)**
   - Original 2002 study that scared everyone about HRT
   - Multiple reanalyses showing HRT safer than originally reported
   - Essential for discussing HRT safety

5. **PubMed (pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov)**
   - Primary database for peer-reviewed medical research
   - Filter by: Review, Meta-Analysis, Clinical Trial
   - Sort by: Most Recent first

### PubMed Search Terms to Use:

**General:**
- "perimenopause" OR "menopausal transition" OR "climacteric"
- "menopause symptoms"
- "perimenopause prevalence"

**Specific symptoms:**
- "perimenopause hot flashes"
- "perimenopause cognitive function"
- "perimenopause depression"
- "perimenopause sleep disturbances"

**Treatment:**
- "menopausal hormone therapy" OR "hormone replacement therapy"
- "HRT safety"
- "exercise perimenopause"
- "mindfulness menopause"

**Filters to apply:**
- Article type: Review, Systematic Review, Meta-Analysis (for best evidence)
- Publication date: Last 10 years (unless it's a landmark study)
- Free full text: If you don't have journal access
